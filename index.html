Who We Are

We are Amoriem Labs, Yale's first and only video game development club. Led entirely by students, we unite passionate gamers, programmers, artists, musicians, storytellers, and designers to bring digital worlds to life.

Amoriem was founded in 2019 and has been making video games ever since. We are the place for your game design ideas and visions.
