Games
Below is the list of games developed by Amoriem Labs! Each game is pitched by students at the beginning of the year, which is then developed throughout the school year.
2024-2025
Boola Dash
Boola Dash is a side-scrolling platformer where you play as Handsome Dan running across Yale trying to avoid obstacles like benches, cones, and students! Play to get on our leaderboard, where the best Resco will win! This game was made in collaberation with the Alumni Fund at Yale.
Made by the Alumni Game team, led by Maryeva Gonzalez. This game will be made available to play soon!!
Encore!
Begin your career at the Multiversal Institute for the Rhythmic Arts (MIRA), an interdimensional school where music, magic, and friendship collide. Recruit new friends from across realities and times to form a band that will rival the ages and unlock the power of rhythm with your bonds. Can your band make the audience scream Encore!?
Made by the Rhythm/Visual Novel Game team, led by Chris Shia. See full credits in the link below!

Glyphbound
Glyphbound is a roguelite deckbuilder that centers around scribing spells to craft crazy combos! Play as a time-travelling wizard tasked with saving the world from a growing corruption.
Made by the Magic team, led by Jeffrey Tan. See full credits in the link below!
